Drinks

A drink is a liquid intended for human consumption. In addition to their basic function of satisfying thirst, drinks play important roles in human culture. Common types of drinks include plain drinking water, milk, coffee, tea, hot chocolate, juice and soft drinks.

Healthy Food

A healthful diet must include a balance of several food groups, as no single group can provide everything the body needs for good health.

Organic Food

rganic food is food produced by methods complying with the standards of organic farming. Standards vary worldwide, but organic farming features practices that cycle resources, promote ecological balance, and conserve biodiversity

White Meat

The use of the terms “white”, “red”, “light” and “dark” in reference to meat may have different meanings and implications based on the context. In culinary terms, white meat is meat which is pale in color before and after cooking.

Sea Food

Seafood is any form of sea life regarded as food by humans, prominently including fish and shellfish. Shellfish include various species of molluscs, crustaceans, and echinoderms
